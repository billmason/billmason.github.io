Silent Enemy and Cold Front are reversed from their airing order, per Memory Alpha's calculation:

The reason for this is because Silent Enemy is explicitly said to occur on September 1, whereas Cold Front is said to happen four months, four weeks, and six days since Enterprise left Earth. As the Rigel X events ended sixteen hours before the April 16th log entry in Broken Bow, and this occurred three days into the mission by Marauders, launch was therefore April 12 or 13, which puts Cold Front on either September 8 or 9.
