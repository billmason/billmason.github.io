Star Trek Chronological Order Viewing List

What is this?

This is where I'm compiling a list of Star Trek TV episodes and movies in the order they occur in the show's internal chronology. A few sites have done this before, such as The Ultimate Chronological Star Trek Viewing Guide and The Star Trek Chronology Project. But I found both of those unsatisfying or lacking or inaccurate for assorted reasons, so I'm working on my own take. Who knows, maybe some fan will find it interesting one day.

Silent Enemy and Cold Front are reversed from their airing order, per Memory Alpha's calculation:

The reason for this is because Silent Enemy is explicitly said to occur on September 1, whereas Cold Front is said to happen four months, four weeks, and six days since Enterprise left Earth. As the Rigel X events ended sixteen hours before the April 16th log entry in Broken Bow, and this occurred three days into the mission by Marauders, launch was therefore April 12 or 13, which puts Cold Front on either September 8 or 9.

23nd Century

Early 2230's

This marks the appearance of the first Short Treks episode. As the episodes of this series by design jump all around in Star Trek history and aren't designed to be watched sequentially as one ongoing story like a traditional series, they will appear scattered throughout the timeline.

The Girl Who Made the Stars
Short Treks season 2 episode 5

The exact year cannot be determined. Michael Burnham was born in 2226. When the character appears in 2236 in Brother it makes it clear that Burnham in The Girl Who Made the Stars falls somewhere in between in age, so early 2230's seems a fair approximation.

2256

The first new series of the Paramount+ era, Discovery, starts here and stays in this 23rd century timeframe for its first two seasons. Designed as a serialized show in a way that Star Trek hadn't done since the later seasons of Deep Space Nine, the viewing order of episodes matches the order they were aired. A few Short Treks episodes insert themselves into the timeline along the way.
